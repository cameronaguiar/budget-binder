The Story Behind Budget Binder

In September 2026, I moved from the United States to Spain to begin a new chapter of my life. Living abroad meant learning how to budget differently, so I started carrying around a paper budget binder where I wrote down every purchase I made. Every coffee, grocery trip, metro ride, and bill was carefully recorded by hand so I could stay on track with my financial goals.

While it worked, I constantly wished there was a simple app that felt just like my notebook—but automatically calculated everything for me. I wanted something that was clean, organized, completely free, and easy enough that anyone could use it.

That idea became Budget Binder. Every account total updates automatically as transactions are added, making it easy to see exactly where your money is going without doing any manual math. It's paper-free, eco-friendly, secure, and designed to make budgeting feel simple instead of stressful.

Building Budget Binder also became my opportunity to learn full-stack software development. This project challenged me to work across multiple programming languages, databases, cloud hosting, authentication, and responsive web design. I'm excited to share it with anyone who wants a simple place to organize their finances, build better habits, and work toward their financial goals.
